KISS PD ratio calculator

This is very simple script to help with tuning KISS flight controller. It is based on the "half - 30%" rule where D = (10*P) / 2 - 30%

Also P is 15-20% higher on Pitch axis usually as there is more momentum to carry. Depends on the quad frame (usually applies to bus style with top mounted battery).
